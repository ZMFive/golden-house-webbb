Our Story

A Place Built for Forever

Golden House is more than a venue — it is where lifelong stories begin. Nestled in a grand estate surrounded by lush gardens and gilded halls, we have been crafting extraordinary weddings since 2010.

Every detail is orchestrated with care: from the sweeping ballroom adorned with crystal chandeliers to the intimate garden ceremonies at sunset. Our team ensures your special day is nothing short of perfection.

We offer bespoke packages tailored to every vision, every culture, and every budget — because every love story is unique.
